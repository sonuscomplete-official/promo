Benefits

Afterpay is an online payment service that allows you to pay for your entire order in 4 fortnightly installments.

  • No waiting - send your gift now
  • Affordable - repayment in 4 fortnightly installments
  • Quick - 2 minute signup
  • No interest or sign-up fee

How does Afterpay work?

Afterpay covers the cost for your entire online order, including shipping

  1. Place an order online
  2. Choose Afterpay as the payment option
  3. Signup or use your existing Afterpay account
  4. Once approved, we will send your gift

Limitations

Placing an order with Afterpay is simple, just select Afterpay in the checkout. Please note, there are some limitations with Afterpay orders:

  • Orders must be less than $1000, including shipping
  • Afterpay cannot be used as a payment method over the phone
