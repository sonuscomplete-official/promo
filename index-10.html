Covid-19 Policies and Procedures

As the global outbreak of Coronavirus (COVID-19) continues to evolve throughout the world, our team at VIP Flowers are taking steps to minimise the risk of exposure to our homeland, here in Australia.

We've introduced additional health and safety protocols to take care of our team, valued customers and lucky recipients.

These recommendations have been implemented with regard to suggestions made by the World Health Organisation and Australian Government bodies, including but not limited to:

  • Delivery drivers have been given 'authority to leave' for all deliveries, and will leave gifts in a safe spot if available
  • Delivery drivers will still contact the recipient on delivery by knocking on the door or providing their name at reception
  • Delivery drivers are regularly sanitising hands during delivery runs
  • Additional workspace cleaning and staff hygiene practices across all of our team
  • Expanding physical space between all of our team, as well as our floral and office workstations
  • Ceasing staff movements between all of our locations
  • Mandatory self-isolation periods for all staff returning from overseas travel
  • All meetings with external parties conducted online where possible or off-site
  • Discontinuing all business travel for employees

We're committed to ensuring we continue to deliver the most beautiful premium flowers to our customers!

How to Order Online

  1. Search our range to find the perfect arrangement and click “Buy Now”.
  2. Begin by either logging in as a registered customer, become a registered customer or continue with the purchase without registering.
  3. Next we ask for the name and contact telephone number for the recipient, the type of occasion for which the flowers are being sent and the message you would like on the card. Click “Continue”. Please note that providing the first and surname of the recipient can be helpful when delivering your order.
  4. The delivery address details for the recipient are then required.  Please ensure you provide the correct name and address details to ensure prompt delivery of your order.  At this point we will also advise the earliest delivery date for your order and request your preference for delivery if the recipient is not in attendance at the time of delivery.
  5. Before proceeding, please read our Delivery Policy.  Other services that may be of interest will also be displayed including morning and afternoon guaranteed delivery options.  If you do not choose these services, your order will be delivered in line with our standard delivery guarantee.
  6. If you have a promotional code you may enter it at the bottom of this page. Click “Continue”.
  7. Provide your details, enter your first and surname, country, contact phone number and email address and click “Continue”.
  8. To proceed with the purchase please provide your credit card details and then press “Place order”.
  9. The next page will be a confirmation of your order with a reference number. Please make a note of this reference number. We will also send you a confirmation email.

Product and Service Enquiries

At VIP Flowers we pride ourselves on providing the highest quality products and services, so if you are not completely happy with your purchase, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery to report any product or service issues.  

We guarantee our arrangements will last 3 to 4 days and recommend watering all arrangement types,  cutting the stems of flowers from bouquets on an angle before arranging in a vase and changing the water in the vase every two days to extend the life of your arrangement. Air conditioning, extreme heat or changes in climate may also have an adverse effect on the flowers.

Please remember that flowers are a natural and perishable product and as such, may be affected by both indoor and outdoor environmental factors, seasonal availability and variations in colour, shape and size.  

For product quality issues, we ask that you provide photos of the arrangement, as this helps us determine how the fault occurred and the best way to rectify the situation.

Any enquiries received later than 2 weeks after delivery will be considered on an individual basis.
