Freshness Guarantee

Quality guarantee – Five day freshness

At VIP Flowers we pride ourselves on quality. Our methods of acquiring fresh stems means that we often have a 24 hour turnaround from the flowers being cut to when they reach our florists' cool rooms. As our florists are experienced and passionate, they will always ensure that quality stems will be used ensuring their creations last. By using air-conditioned vans the flowers will arrive as fresh as when they left the florist. This is why we offer a five day freshness guarantee because we believe in our products being long lasting and high quality. Using specified designs and ingredient lists, our products will always be picture perfect to what you can see online.
